Publications making use of JutulDarcy.jl ​

This page lists papers that use JutulDarcy.jl. Do you have a paper that is not listed here? Feel free to make a pull request, or send an e-mail with the paper title and link.

JutulDarcy.jl - a Fully Differentiable High-Performance Reservoir Simulator based on Automatic Differentiation. O. Møyner. Computational Geosciences (2025) is the main paper on JutulDarcy.jl. Please cite this if you make use of this package in your work.
